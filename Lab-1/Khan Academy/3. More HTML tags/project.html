  1. Steak

Steak bites with mashed potatoes

  • Time: 1:30 hours
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Beef steak 1 piece
Potatoes 3 medium
Butter 2 tbsp
Garlic 2 cloves
Milk ½ cup

Step 1: Prepare the potatoes – Peel and boil potatoes until soft (about 15 minutes).

Step 2: Mash the potatoes – Drain, then mash with butter and warm milk until smooth.

Step 3: Season the steak – Rub steak with salt, pepper, and minced garlic.

Step 4: Cook the steak – Sear in a hot pan for 3–5 minutes per side (depending on doneness).

Step 5: Serve – Plate the steak with mashed potatoes and enjoy!
